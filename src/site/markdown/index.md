## Welcome Linux Users

The Houston Linux User Group is the place for Linux activity in Houston. Whether you
are looking to socialize with Linux loving peers, promote the adoption of Linux in a
particular venue, or learn about Linux and its software offerings, the Houston Linux
User Group can assist you in achieving your goals, with Linux!

The Houston Linux User Group's mission is based on four pillars:

- **Advocacy** - Getting Linux in front of people.
- **Education** - Teaching people more Linux.
- **Support** - Assisting people who need help.
- **Socializing** - Having fun with our Linux loving peers.

### About

The Houston Linux Users Group was founded in 1995. With 31 years of computing history,
the group has transitioned with the needs of the computing community.

The most active computer group in Houston, the Houston Linux Users Group meets weekly,
barring holidays, hurricanes, and planned shutdowns.  

With a breadth and depth of computing experience rarely found elsewhere, the group has
helped thousands of members use and leverage Linux for work and play.
